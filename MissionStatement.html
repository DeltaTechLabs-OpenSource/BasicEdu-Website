Mission Statement

Inclusivity in Technology Enabled Education

Posted by Debargha Ganguly on December 27, 2018

Education is the single most powerful tool right now that is solely responsible for shaping a child’s perspective on all facets of life. It has become critical that every child has access to a certain quality of education irrespective of their level of proficiency and where they live. In order to make technology enabled education become a reality to them, we offer a device that can be plugged into any television set and turn the same into a computer. The design is open source and anyone with the right skills can build one. As an alternative, our changemaker partners can build one for you, for absolutely the cost of the parts.

Technology in education is proven to mould the patterns of thinking and reinforce experiential learning amongst all age groups. It’s essential for children to have hands on experience with computers, irrespective of what they want to pursue. The real impact that this technology can have on lives is being tested as we speak through out pilot programmes, and promising results indicate the pressing need to integrate this into all curriculums We remain committed towards making computers accessible to all.
