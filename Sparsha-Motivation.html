Personal Motivation : Sparsha Mishra

What inspires me.

Posted by Sparsha Mishra on January 07, 2018

What inspires me to be a part of BasicEdu is that the fact that more people are realizing how important it has become that we make technology-enabled education a reality. Most people have an idea and fail to implement the same into reality and to witness someone so young, carrying forward this ideology is what makes me happy to be a part of the team. There are so many children out there filled with knowledge and creativity but are unable to work their way out due to the inaccessibility to computers. We do not realize that as we live in the comfort of our house and fail to broaden our mind to see beyond just that.

As I grew up, technology wasn't as advanced as it is today. I couldn't afford a computer of my own other than using the one at my Father’s office. Although the time was different and all I wanted to do was play games on the computer but as I became older, the importance of having a computer came to light. Be it be for studying for exams, looking for relevant information on Google, and slowly computer became an inseparable part of me. It is when you have something that you fail to see what others don’t. That is the most challenging part that you look beyond yourself. With this mindset to be able to provide children with computers that they can afford is what makes me happy to be a part of this team. I am sure that this idea is going to help hundreds of thousands all over in pursuing their dreams and making them a reality like ours.
